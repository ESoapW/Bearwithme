About Us

Our aim is to let more people know and pay attention to children with autism and become more tolerant of them.

Compared with peers, autistic children live in a world full of loneliness, sadness and difficulties.
Even though it is still impossible to explain the cause of the disease, and there is no panacea that can completely cured it.
We still hope to make them feel the respect and care of the society as much as possible.
